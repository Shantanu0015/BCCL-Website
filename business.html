Business of BCCL
Company Overview

Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited, is the major producer of prime coking coal in India. Established in 1972, BCCL operates 36 coal mines in the Jharia and Raniganj coalfields, which are the only sources of prime coking coal in the country. The company is committed to supplying high-quality coal to the steel sector and other core industries, supporting the nation's industrial growth.

Products & Services
  • Prime Coking Coal: Used primarily in steel manufacturing.
  • Medium Coking Coal: Supplied to various metallurgical industries.
  • Washed Coal: Processed in washeries for improved quality and reduced ash content.
  • By-products: Includes coal tar, ammonium sulphate, and other chemicals from coal processing.
  • Coal Beneficiation: Upgradation of coal quality through advanced beneficiation plants.
Major Customers
  • Integrated Steel Plants (SAIL, RINL, Tata Steel, etc.)
  • Power Utilities
  • Cement and Fertilizer Industries
  • Other core sector industries
Marketing & Distribution

BCCL has a robust marketing and distribution network, ensuring timely supply of coal to customers across India. The company uses rail, road, and conveyor systems for efficient coal dispatch. E-auction platforms are also used for transparent and competitive coal sales.

Sustainability & CSR
  • Environmental Initiatives: Land reclamation, afforestation, and pollution control measures.
  • Community Development: Healthcare, education, and infrastructure projects for local communities.
  • Safety: Implementation of best practices and regular training to maintain a safe working environment.
  • Innovation: Adoption of new technologies and automation for improved productivity and resource management.

Business Highlights
  • Largest Producer: BCCL is one of the largest producers of coking coal in India, contributing significantly to the nation’s steel industry.
  • Strategic Location: Mines are strategically located in the heart of India’s industrial belt, ensuring efficient supply chains.
  • Export Potential: Exploring opportunities to export high-quality coking coal to international markets.
  • Joint Ventures: Collaborating with national and international partners for technology transfer and business growth.

BCCL continues to innovate and expand its business operations, focusing on sustainable growth, technological advancement, and value creation for all stakeholders. The company’s commitment to excellence ensures its position as a trusted partner in India’s industrial progress.
