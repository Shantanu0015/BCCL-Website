Finance at BCCL
Finance Overview

The Finance department at Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) is responsible for managing the company’s financial planning, budgeting, and reporting. It ensures the financial health of the organization and supports strategic decision-making for sustainable growth and value creation.

Financial Management & Planning
  • Budgeting: Preparation of annual budgets and monitoring of expenditures for optimal resource allocation.
  • Financial Planning: Long-term financial strategies to support business expansion and modernization.
  • Cost Control: Implementation of cost reduction measures and efficiency improvements.
  • Investment Management: Evaluation and execution of capital investment projects.
Revenue & Sales
  • Revenue generation through coal sales to steel plants, power utilities, and other industries.
  • Transparent e-auction platforms for coal sales.
  • Efficient billing and collection systems to ensure timely revenue realization.
Audit & Compliance
  • Regular internal and external audits to ensure transparency and accountability.
  • Compliance with statutory regulations, tax laws, and corporate governance standards.
  • Implementation of financial controls to prevent fraud and errors.
Digital Initiatives
  • Adoption of ERP and IT solutions for real-time financial monitoring and reporting.
  • Online payment and e-procurement systems for efficiency and transparency.
Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
  • Allocation of funds for community development, healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.
  • Transparent reporting of CSR expenditures and outcomes.
