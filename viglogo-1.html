Vigilance at BCCL
Vigilance Overview

The Vigilance Department at Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) is committed to promoting transparency, integrity, and accountability within the organization. Vigilance ensures that all operations are conducted ethically and in compliance with rules and regulations, fostering a culture of honesty and good governance.

Functions of Vigilance
  • Preventive, detective, and punitive vigilance activities.
  • Surprise inspections, audits, and system studies to identify and mitigate risks.
  • Processing complaints and conducting investigations into alleged irregularities.
  • Advising management on system improvements to reduce the scope for corruption.
Preventive Vigilance
  • Identification of sensitive areas and processes prone to corruption.
  • Regular review and simplification of procedures to enhance transparency.
  • Training and awareness programs for employees on ethical conduct and anti-corruption measures.
  • Implementation of e-governance and digital monitoring tools.
Complaint Redressal Mechanism
  • Multiple channels for employees and stakeholders to report grievances and unethical practices confidentially.
  • Online complaint portal and dedicated vigilance helpline.
  • Prompt investigation and resolution of complaints.
  • Protection of whistleblowers as per government guidelines.
Vigilance Awareness & Outreach
  • Observance of Vigilance Awareness Week with seminars, workshops, and competitions.
  • Distribution of awareness materials and display of posters across BCCL units.
  • Engagement with schools, colleges, and the public to spread the message of integrity.
Achievements & Initiatives
  • Implementation of online file tracking and e-procurement systems.
  • Reduction in processing time for contracts and payments.
  • Recognition for best practices in vigilance and transparency.
