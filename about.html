About Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL)

Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) is a Public Sector Undertaking engaged in mining of coal and allied activities. It was incorporated in January, 1972 to operate coking coal mines operating in the Jharia & Raniganj Coalfields, taken over by the Government of India on 16th Oct, 1971 to ensure planned development of the scarce coking coal resources in the country.

BCCL is a subsidiary of Coal India Limited, with its headquarters at Dhanbad, Jharkhand. The company operates 36 coal mines, including both underground and opencast mines, in the Jharia and Raniganj coalfields of India. BCCL is the major producer of prime coking coal in the country, catering to the requirements of the steel sector and other core industries.

  • Core Activities: Mining of coking and non-coking coal, coal beneficiation, and supply to steel plants, power utilities, and other industries.
  • Operational Area: Jharia and Raniganj coalfields, the only sources of prime coking coal in India.
  • Workforce: Employs around 39,000 people (as per latest data).
  • Infrastructure: Manages several coal washeries, workshops, hospitals, and educational institutions for employees and their families.
Vision & Mission
  • Vision: To emerge as a global player in the primary energy sector committed to provide energy security to the country by attaining environmentally & socially sustainable growth through best practices from mine to market.
  • Mission: To produce and market planned quantity of coal and coal products efficiently and economically in an eco-friendly manner with due regard to safety, conservation and quality.
Key Achievements
  • Major supplier of prime coking coal to the steel sector in India.
  • Implementation of modern mining technologies and safety standards.
  • Active in environmental management, land reclamation, and community development.
  • Consistent focus on employee welfare, training, and skill development.
Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

BCCL is committed to the welfare of local communities through initiatives in healthcare, education, infrastructure, and environmental sustainability. The company runs hospitals, schools, and vocational training centers for the benefit of employees and society at large.
