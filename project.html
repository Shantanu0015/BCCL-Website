Operations at BCCL
Operational Overview

Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) operates in the Jharia and Raniganj coalfields, the only sources of prime coking coal in India. The company manages a mix of underground and open-cast mines, washeries, and a robust logistics network to ensure efficient coal production and supply.

Mining Operations
  • Underground Mines: BCCL operates several underground mines using conventional and mechanized mining methods.
  • Open-cast Mines: Large-scale open-cast mining for higher productivity and efficient coal extraction.
  • Mechanization: Deployment of continuous miners, longwall mining, and other advanced equipment for improved safety and output.
  • Production Planning: Scientific mine planning and scheduling for optimal resource utilization.
Coal Washeries & Beneficiation
  • 8 coal washeries for beneficiation and quality upgradation.
  • Production of washed coking coal with reduced ash content for steel plants.
  • By-products such as middlings and coal fines supplied to power and other industries.
Logistics & Dispatch
  • Integrated rail, road, and conveyor systems for coal transportation.
  • Computerized weighbridges and monitoring for accurate dispatch.
  • Online coal booking and e-auction platforms for transparent sales.
Safety & Environment
  • Strict adherence to safety standards and regular safety audits.
  • Implementation of environmental management plans, land reclamation, and afforestation.
  • Continuous monitoring of air, water, and noise pollution.
  • Community awareness programs on safety and environment.
